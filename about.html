    Why Suraj Stone Art?

    With a legacy dating back to 1992, Suraj Stone Art has been a trailblazer in the world of natural stone artistry. Our time-tested legacy is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence.

  • Innovative Designs:

    We offer a rich repertoire of architectural stone assemblies, value-added stones, and ornamental products. From traditional to contemporary designs, our innovative creations cater to diverse tastes and preferences.

  • Quality Craftsmanship:

  • Global Presence:

    Suraj Stone Art has a global footprint, making our exceptional stone products accessible to clients around the world. Whether it's renowned hotels, landmark buildings, or private residences, our works adorn spaces with timeless beauty.

  • Commitment to Customer Satisfaction:

    At Suraj Stone Art, customer satisfaction is at the core of our values. We go the extra mile to understand your unique requirements and preferences, ensuring that our stone creations exceed your expectations.

    About Us

    Our establishment

    Established in 1992, Suraj Stone Art has been a pioneering force in the domain of natural stone craftsmanship. Over the years, our legacy in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, has grown, marking us as a distinguished name in the industry. With a foundation rooted in tradition and a commitment to innovation, our journey reflects a deep-seated passion for the artistry of natural stones. From iconic structures to contemporary designs, our history is etched in the enduring beauty of our creations. Suraj Stone Art stands as a testament to heritage, craftsmanship, and a commitment to timeless elegance.

    Our aim / Dream

    At Suraj Stone Art, our aim and dream converge on the vision of redefining the landscape of natural stone craftsmanship. We aspire to be a beacon of innovation, blending traditional expertise with modern techniques to create architectural marvels. Our dream is to contribute to the global aesthetic with stone structures that tell a story of heritage, creativity, and enduring beauty. We envision Suraj Stone Art as a symbol of excellence, delivering not just products but timeless works of art that resonate with the essence of each stone. With a commitment to sustainable practices, our aim is to preserve and promote the rich legacy of stone carving for generations to come.

    Technology and infrastructure

    Embracing cutting-edge technology, Suraj Stone Art boasts a state-of-the-art infrastructure that redefines the standards of stone craftsmanship. Our advanced machinery and equipment ensure precision in carving and finishing, allowing us to bring out the innate beauty of each natural stone. With a commitment to innovation, we invest in modern tools that complement the traditional craftsmanship we hold dear. Our infrastructure is a testament to our dedication to quality and efficiency, enabling us to meet the evolving demands of the industry. From digital design to precision cutting, our technology-driven approach sets us apart, making Suraj Stone Art a hub of excellence in the realm of natural stone artistry.
